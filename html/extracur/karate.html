Karate

Self-defense opens up the source of freedom for women in an imperfect world. Building confidence in physical resistance skills allows women to feel more capable and more at peace when in potentially dangerous areas as they know the proper techniques to help fend off an attacker.

Considering the Importance of Self-defense, I joined Golden Tiger Karate Training Institute when I was at 9 years old and achieved Black Belt in 3 years later at 12 years old from International Karate Budokan. Besides, I am also active member of UAE Taekwondo and Karate Federation and where I had an opportunity to meet the 1st Dan & 2nd Dan Masters and learn the more techniques of the sport. I have participated in various martial arts training camps organized by the Federation.

I participated in National Karate Championships and Won winners Cups and Certificates in 3 categories: organized by UAE Taekwondo and Karate Federation in Y2016 & Y2017.
