Singing

Bhav Sangeeth in India, basically refers to vocal singing which is popular with a common man and is meant for their entertainment. Pracheen Kala Kendra is one of the oldest and leading cultural organizations of India established in the year 1956. I had continuously practiced and appeared for Bhav Sangeeth Vocal Singing Prarambhik Part-1 & 2 examinations and successfully completed with First Class and Distinction.

I am very proud of learning this vocal Singing skills and this Singing art has instilled an additional confidence in me.

I further developed my skills in the Singing by practicing and Performing the classical singing in stage competitions and also won the prizes. In addition I participated in School Singing auditions and Sing-along Competitions and won the First and Second Prizes

Besides I also practiced and participated in many Stage Singing performances of cultural functions.
